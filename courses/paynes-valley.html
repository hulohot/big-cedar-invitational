Named in honor of the late Payne Stewart, Payne's Valley is the first fully public-access course in the world completed by Tiger Woods' design firm TGR Design. A partnership between Johnny Morris, Tiger Woods and the Payne Stewart Family, this 19-hole championship course showcases the region's natural beauty through dramatic elevation changes and breathtaking Ozark scenery. Named Best New Public Course in America by Golf Digest when it opened in 2021.

Signature Holes
Hole 4 – "Big Rock"
Par 5 • 580 yards • Handicap 1
A massive par 5 that plays uphill to a green framed by a dramatic rock wall. The fairway is wide but strategic, with risk-reward options on the second shot. Three good shots are rewarded; shortcuts are punished.
Hole 8 – "Valley View"
Par 4 • 485 yards • Handicap 3
The signature hole with panoramic views of the Ozark Valley. A slight dogleg right with a demanding tee shot over a ravine. The green is perched on a ledge with dramatic drops on three sides.
Hole 19 – "The Rock"
Par 3 • 175 yards • Bonus Hole
Payne's Valley features a spectacular bonus 19th hole — a par 3 with an island green surrounded by water and rocks. Perfect for settling bets after the round. Tiger's design nod to famous 17th holes everywhere.
