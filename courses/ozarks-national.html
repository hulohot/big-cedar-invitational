The golf course architect team of Coore & Crenshaw share Johnny Morris' passion and appreciation for nature and conservation, making Ozarks National a striking display for the senses and a fitting tribute to the Ozarks region. Designed to test avid players and golf travelers from around the country, this walkable layout winds through native grasses and dramatic rock outcroppings with sweeping vistas of the Ozark Mountains.

Signature Holes
Hole 2 – "The Rock"
Par 4 • 440 yards • Handicap 3
A demanding par 4 with a dramatic rock wall framing the right side. The fairway slopes left to right, and the green is perched on a ridge with severe fall-offs. One of the most photographed holes at Big Cedar.
Hole 9 – "The Closer"
Par 4 • 455 yards • Handicap 1
The toughest hole on the front nine plays uphill to a green guarded by bunkers and native grass. A three-shot hole for most, requiring careful placement to avoid the deep hollows surrounding the green.
Hole 17 – "Fazio's Folly"
Par 4 • 445 yards • Handicap 2
A stunning par 4 with a blind tee shot over a ridge. The approach plays downhill to a multi-tiered green with dramatic views of the surrounding mountains. Accuracy is premium here.
